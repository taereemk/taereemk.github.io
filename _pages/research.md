## Research Overview

My research focuses on understanding and predicting hydroclimatic extremes to support resilient water resources management and hazard mitigation. I develop statistical and machine learning approaches that integrate hydrologic observations, climate information, and remote sensing products to improve prediction and decision-making.

### Research Areas

### Hydroclimatology
Understanding the variability and change of precipitation and hydrologic extremes across spatial and temporal scales.

### Climate Extremes
Investigating heavy rainfall, floods, droughts, and compound events under a changing climate.

### Hydrologic Forecasting
Developing forecasting frameworks that combine physical understanding with statistical and deep learning approaches.

### Machine Learning for Water Resources
Applying deep learning and artificial intelligence to precipitation estimation, rainfall forecasting, and hydrologic prediction.
