I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Civil, Environmental, and Infrastructure Engineering at George Mason University.

My research focuses on hydroclimatology, climate extremes, hydrologic forecasting, and machine learning for water resources.

## Research Interests

- Hydroclimatology
- Climate extremes
- Hydrologic forecasting
- Machine learning
- Remote sensing
